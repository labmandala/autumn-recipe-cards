Spiced Apple Cider

Prep Time: 10 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 20 Min

This warm and sweet beverage has a rich glow and adds some heat and spice to a cool night, alone or with company.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon apple cider
  • 1 small orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 small apple, thinly sliced
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 star anise
  • 1/2 tablespoon whole cloves
  • 2 teaspoons allspice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • dash of ginger

Directions

  1. To a large pot add all ingredients, except extract.
  2. Bring mixture to a boil, then turn down heat and simmer 2 hours.
  3. Remove pan from heat and allow to cool just a little.
  4. Remove fruit and spices from the mixture.
  5. Add the vanilla extract. Optional: spike with 1 1/2 cups rum.
  6. Garnish with a fresh fruit slice and a pinch of ground spice and serve.

Creamy Fig Toast

Prep Time: 5 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 5 Min

This delicious snack is a nourishing treat that is satisfying all by itself or paired with a bowl of oats for a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces seed bread
  • 4 tablespoons creamy cashew spread
  • 2 tablespoons fig preserves
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh figs
  • 1-2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Pinch of chia seeds

Directions

  1. Spread the creamy cashew spread between two pieces of toasted seed bread.
  2. Add a tablespoon of preserves to each piece on top of the cashew spread.
  3. Add the fresh figs to the top of the toast slices.
  4. Drizzle the topped toast with the maple syrup.
  5. Finish off with a sprinkle of cinnamon and chia seeds, as desired. Enjoy.

Maple Cranberry Pecans

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 15 Min

These glazed pecans combine maple syrup, sweet and chewy cranberries, and and hint of pumpkin pie spice for serious snacking.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pecan halves
  • 4 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 3/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • Splash of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of sea salt

Directions

  1. Prefeat oven to 300° Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine all ingredients except salt into a large bowl and stir.
  3. Spread mixture onto a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 10 - 15 minutes to glaze nuts and meld flavors together, without making too toasty.
  5. Allow to cool slightly and serve.
